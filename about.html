    ABOUT

    Computer science is vital in our tech-driven era, Playing a crucial role in leveraging the benefits of information technology for societal progress It involves creating reliable, correct, and ethical application and software systems. Central to this is programming, a skill highly valued by employers.



    The diverse courses equip students with skills for various economic sectors, preparing them for roles in the computing industry, project teams, and even research. It also paves the way for further study or research by introducing them to both established and emerging tech and methodologies. The department offers two undergraduate degrees, a bachelor of science in computer science, both as a single-major and major-minor and bachelor of science in information technology.
